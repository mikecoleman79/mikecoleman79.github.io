Member Reporting Platform with Advanced Forms & Data Management

Role: Senior WordPress Developer
Agency: Popcorn Web Design
Client: Financial services / member organisation (confidential)

WordPress Gravity Forms PHP JavaScript Custom Post Types DataTables Access Control

Overview

I led the redevelopment of a member reporting platform for a financial services organisation, replacing a manual Excel-based reporting process with a secure, web-based system built on WordPress.

The platform allows member organisations (banks) to submit annual and bi-annual financial reports through a private members area, with structured data capture, validation, persistence and reporting tools for administrators.

The Problem

The client’s existing workflow relied on large, complex Excel spreadsheets to collect financial data from members. These were manually completed, emailed back and processed offline.

Key issues included:

  • High risk of data entry errors and inconsistent formatting
  • No ability to partially complete and return to submissions
  • Manual validation and checking of financial data
  • Static tables on the website with no live reporting
  • No secure, member-specific access to historical submissions

The client needed a scalable, repeatable reporting system that could replace Excel entirely while remaining easy for non-technical users to complete.

Constraints

  • Complex financial models had to be accurately replicated online
  • Reports needed to support both annual and bi-annual submissions
  • Members could only access and view their own data
  • The system had to support drafts, saved progress and resuming later
  • Submitted data needed to be reusable for reporting and export

Solution

Private Members Area & Access Control

  • Creation of a dedicated members-only user role
  • Page-level and data-level access restrictions
  • Users mapped to specific member organisations
  • Members restricted to viewing and editing their own reports only

Advanced Multi-Step Reporting Form

  • Multi-step Gravity Form designed to mirror the existing Excel spreadsheet
  • Save and continue functionality allowing users to return later
  • Custom field validation for financial inputs
  • Summary and review step prior to final submission
  • Automated email notifications on save and submission

Data Storage & Modelling

  • Custom post type for members
  • Custom post type for member reports
  • Each form submission stored as structured, queryable data
  • Clear relationship between users, members and reports

Reporting, Tables & Exports

  • Admin-facing reporting tables powered by DataTables.js
  • Filtering, sorting and searching across submissions
  • Export of member reports to CSV for analysis
  • Export of individual member reports to PDF for records and compliance

Admin & Operational Tooling

  • Backend views for saved (draft) and submitted reports
  • Manual entry tools for testing and data verification
  • Clear separation between submission, review and reporting workflows

Outcome

  • Fully replaced Excel-based reporting with a secure web platform
  • Improved data accuracy through validation and structured inputs
  • Reduced manual administration and follow-up with members
  • Created a scalable reporting system for future reporting cycles
  • Enabled members to manage and review their submissions independently
