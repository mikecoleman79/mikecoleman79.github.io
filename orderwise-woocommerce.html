B2B WooCommerce Platform with Orderwise CRM Integration

Role: Senior / Lead WordPress Developer
Agency: Popcorn Web Design
Client: Healthcare / B2B e-commerce (NDA)

WordPress WooCommerce ACF PHP JavaScript Orderwise CRM

Overview

I led the technical delivery of a highly bespoke WooCommerce platform for a healthcare B2B client requiring complex pricing, ordering and compliance workflows.

The project centred around a deep integration with Orderwise CRM, supporting customer-specific price lists, stock visibility, multi-store accounts and strict ordering rules — functionality well beyond standard WooCommerce capabilities.

The Problem

The client needed to replace manual and fragmented ordering processes with a single, self-serve e-commerce platform that accurately reflected their existing Orderwise data.

Core challenges included:

  • Customer-specific pricing and product availability
  • Multiple stores and user profiles per account
  • Regulatory requirements for controlled products
  • Large product catalogue with live stock indicators

Off-the-shelf WooCommerce plugins could not support this level of custom logic without compromising performance or stability.

Constraints

  • Orderwise acted as the single source of truth for customers, pricing and stock
  • Complex customer group logic (e.g. pharmacies vs GPs)
  • Third-party systems outside our direct control
  • Platform required ongoing use during development

Solution

Orderwise Integration & Data Handling

  • Custom database configuration to store raw Orderwise data
  • Import scripts to synchronise pricing, stock and customer records
  • Scheduled update logic for store details and price lists
  • Custom order export logic to return structured data back to Orderwise

Customer Groups & Pricing Logic

  • Dynamic customer-specific price lists mapped from Orderwise
  • Minimum spend thresholds and maximum order quantities
  • Restricted product visibility per customer group
  • Different site experiences depending on customer type

Custom Order & Basket Workflows

  • Profile switcher enabling multiple baskets per customer account
  • Daily order limits with paused / pending order states
  • Automated abandoned basket notifications for customers and admins
  • Buying lists and purchasing restrictions per group

Search, Stock & UX Enhancements

  • AJAX-powered product search with live stock indicators
  • Customer-aware pricing displayed throughout search and listings
  • Drug tariff (RRP) pricing shown alongside customer pricing
  • Slide-out basket, offer notifications and promotional popups

Account & Compliance Features

  • Controlled drug requisition forms within the customer portal
  • Back-in-stock email notifications
  • CQF onboarding process with custom registration logic
  • Live chat integration for customer support

Outcome

  • Stable, scalable B2B e-commerce platform tightly integrated with Orderwise
  • Reduced manual administration and order processing overhead
  • Clear separation between data, business logic and presentation
  • Platform capable of evolving alongside complex business requirements
